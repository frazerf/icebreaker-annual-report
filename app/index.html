Seven days, one tee, zero washes

#teesforgood

Our #teesforgood campaign encouraged our community to move to natural by challenging them to get more mileage from their tee, and, in turn, reduce impact on the planet.

With up to 152L of water used per wash, and up to 700,000 microfibers released¹, washing clothes frequently may have a significant impact on the planet. The icebreaker seven-day t-shirt challenge encouraged people to try wearing the same tee for seven days without washing it. A total of 2.7 million people engaged with the campaign and 57,000 people participated in the t-shirt challenge. By breaking the cycle, we estimate they collectively prevented 750 million microfibers from entering the ocean.

We demonstrated how small steps can produce big changes. More wear, less impact
